Trainwrecks esports

Who we are

Trainwrecks eSports is a Finnish esports organization founded in 2025, driven by strong vision, a clear and determined goal, and passionate team.

Our aim is to build one of the biggest and most recognized esports brands in Finland - and our journey has only begun.

Our mission is clear: we strive to combine top-level competitive gaming, high-quality content creation, and collaborative approach into the unified whole.

We are building an organization that doesn't just participate - but leaves its mark on the Finnish esports scene.

Tag along for the ride and help us build our legacy!

It's time to derail the competition.
