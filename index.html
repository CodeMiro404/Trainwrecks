Who we are

We are Trainwrecks eSports - a competitive gaming organization built on raw passion and relentless drive. Founded in 2025 by Junantuoma Club association, our team thrives to create a memorable brand and community.

At our core, we're not just a team, but a community. At Trainwrecks eSports we believe in creating safe and inclusive space where every player can thrive and evolve. We're committed to supporting young talent, opening doors for the next generation of pros, creators and eSports leaders.

Our mission is to build and maintain a sustainable winning culture and create new opportunities for everyone involved, and most importantly, have fun while doing it!

From grind to glory - We are Trainwrecks and it's time to derail the competition.
